## Partner Integration Roadmap for World Monitor

### Target Integrations

1. **Twilio**
   - **Rationale**: Enhance communication capabilities by integrating SMS and voice alerts for world events.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 5,000 SMS messages/month.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Enables users to receive real-time alerts on critical world events, improving responsiveness.

2. **OpenWeatherMap**
   - **Rationale**: Provide real-time weather data to enrich world monitoring insights.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 1,000 calls/month for current weather data.
   - **Integration effort**: Small (S).
   - **Value-add**: Users can correlate weather conditions with world events, enhancing situational awareness.

3. **Zapier**
   - **Rationale**: Allow users to automate workflows between World Monitor and other applications.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 100 tasks/month.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Simplifies the process of integrating with other tools, increasing user productivity.

4. **Google Maps API**
   - **Rationale**: Visualize world events on a map for better geographical context.
   - **Free-tier limits**: $200 credit/month (approx. 28,000 map loads).
   - **Integration effort**: Large (L).
   - **Value-add**: Users can see the geographical impact of events, aiding in analysis and decision-making.

5. **Eventbrite**
   - **Rationale**: Integrate event management for world events and conferences.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Free for events with free tickets.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Users can create and manage events related to world monitoring, fostering community engagement.

6. **Stripe**
   - **Rationale**: Facilitate payments for premium features or subscriptions.
   - **Free-tier limits**: No monthly fees; pay per transaction.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Streamlines the monetization of premium services, enhancing revenue potential.

7. **Slack**
   - **Rationale**: Provide real-time notifications and updates through a popular team collaboration tool.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 5 integrations and 10,000 messages searchable.
   - **Integration effort**: Small (S).
   - **Value-add**: Keeps teams informed and engaged with real-time updates on world events.

8. **AWS Lambda**
   - **Rationale**: Enable serverless computing for scalable event processing.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 1 million requests/month.
   - **Integration effort**: Large (L).
   - **Value-add**: Improves system scalability and efficiency, allowing for rapid processing of world event data.

### Summary
Integrating with these partners will enhance the functionality of the World Monitor product, address user pain points, and create opportunities for revenue generation through affiliate programs and premium features. Prioritizing integrations with clear value-adds and manageable integration efforts will ensure a successful rollout.
