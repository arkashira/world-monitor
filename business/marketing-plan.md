# Marketing Plan
## Positioning
World Monitor: "All-in-one monitoring solution for developers and users to track global events and stay informed."
Alternative positioning statements:
1. "Real-time world monitoring for proactive decision-making."
2. "Comprehensive event tracking for a more informed world."
3. "Your window to the world: stay updated, stay ahead."

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
1. **Alex Chen**: Developer, daily job involves integrating APIs for event tracking, budget $1,000/month.
2. **Maya Patel**: Researcher, daily job requires monitoring global events for trend analysis, budget $500/month.
3. **Ethan Kim**: Business owner, daily job involves staying informed about global market trends, budget $2,000/month.

## Channels
Top 3 channels with estimated Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):
1. **Social Media (Twitter, LinkedIn)**: $15/CAC
2. **Influencer Marketing (Developer communities)**: $30/CAC
3. **Paid Advertising (Google Ads)**: $20/CAC

## Content Cadence
Week-by-week content plan:
Week 1-2: Introduction to World Monitor, features, and benefits
Week 3-4: User testimonials, case studies, and success stories
Week 5-6: Tutorial series, "Getting started with World Monitor"
Week 7-8: Advanced features, API integration, and customization options
Week 9-10: Community engagement, Q&A sessions, and feedback collection
Week 11-12: Launch preparation, finalizing marketing materials, and press releases
Week 13: Launch day, promotional campaigns, and special offers

## Launch Milestones
Key milestones for the 90-day go-to-market plan:
* **D-30**: Finalize marketing materials, launch website, and start social media campaigns
* **D-0**: Launch World Monitor, press release, and influencer marketing campaigns
* **D+30**: Analyze user feedback, iterate on features, and optimize marketing channels
* **D+90**: Review success metrics, plan for future development, and explore new markets

## Success Metrics
Target metrics for the 90-day go-to-market plan:
* **DAU (Daily Active Users)**: 1,000 by D+90
* **$MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)**: $50,000 by D+90
